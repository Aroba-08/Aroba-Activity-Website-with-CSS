WELCOME TO STARDEW

You've inherited your grandfather's old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down
tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life. Can you learn to live off the land
and turn these overgrown fields into a thriving home? It won't be easy. Ever since Joja
Corporation came to town, the old ways of life have all but disappeared. The community center,
once the town's most vibrant hub of activity, now lies in shambles. But the valley seems full
of opportunity. With a little dedication, you might just be the one to restore Stardew
Valley to greatness!

Stardew Valley is a simulation role-playing video game developed by Eric "ConcernedApe"
Barone. Players take the role of a character who inherits their deceased grandfather's
dilapidated farm in a place known as Stardew Valley. The game was released for Windows
in February 2016 before being ported to other platforms.
